Score Analysis:

Effortlessly analyze and gain insights from your assessment scores with this AI-powered data analysis tool! Tailored for teachers, this form allows you to generate a custom prompt to analyze data from assessments like standardized tests or summative assignments. Simply input your grade, subject, assessment type, maximum score, and your students' scores to obtain a comprehensive analysis, uncovering trends and providing actionable recommendations for improvement. Transform the way you approach data analysis and enhance the learning experience for your students with this intuitive tool!

Instructions:

  1. Customize the prompt filling out the form
  2. Click "Generate Prompt"
  3. Wait for the unique prompt to generate
  4. Click "Copy Prompt"
  5. Paste the prompt into ChatGPT
  6. See the results!
  7. Type "continue" into the chat if the AI's answer ends before it is complete

  4. Distribution: The scores are not normally distributed. There are a few high scores and a few low scores, but most of the scores are in the middle range.
  5. Outliers: There are two scores that are significantly lower than the rest of the data set: 5 and 12. These scores may be considered outliers.

  3. Use the quartiles to group students and provide targeted instruction and resources to help them improve their scores.
  4. Encourage students who scored higher than the mean score to continue to challenge themselves and strive for even higher scores.
